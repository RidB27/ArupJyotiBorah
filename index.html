About Me

I am a motivated individual passionate about economic empowerment, rural development, and capacity building in marginalized communities. My academic journey reflects this dedication, spanning diverse fields to create a positive impact. With a Biotechnology undergraduate degree, I specialized in Biotechnology, Microbiology, and Chemistry. Pursuing an MSC in Applied Microbiology at VIT University, I explored practical applications of my interests. PG Diplomas in Intellectual Property Rights and Criminology enriched my perspective.
I expanded into Geoinformatics, intertwining geospatial insights with life sciences, environment, and waste management. This fusion unveils new ecological perspectives, sustainable strategies, and waste mitigation. A PhD in biowaste valorization from IIT Guwahati defines my passion for sustainable solutions, converting waste biomass into biofuels and biopolymers. I am committed to circular economy and climate action, aligned with Sustainable Development Goals.
Recognized with the Young Scientist Award 2021 and National Award on Technology Innovation, I hold impactful publications with an average impact factor of 6. Pursuing Environmental Science and Sustainable Rural Development, I aim to contribute to positive change. An expert in Bioprocess and Fermentation, I advocate for global challenges like climate change.
Rooted in grassroots innovation and with few distinct recognition, I founded the IIT Guwahati Research Conclave, promoting collaboration among students, industry leaders, and policymakers. With a vision to improve marginalized lives, I am prepared to participate in campaigns, projects, and collaborations for sustainable goals. My journey embodies knowledge pursuit, sustainable commitment, and transforming communities.
